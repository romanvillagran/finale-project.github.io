Ratatouille

Ratatouille first originated in the Eighteenth century south-east of france and is a summer vegetable "stew". The "stew" consist of courgettes, aubergines, red and yellow peppers, tomatoes, and onions, other vegetables can be added. The dish is made up of aromatic herbs and lots of vegetables.

According to sources. The reason why Ratatouille exist is because the farmers need something to do with overripe vegetables so they began to slow cooked these vegetable which resulted to the creation of Ratatouille
