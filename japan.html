Japanese sushi

Sushi first originated in Japan in the eight century. Back then sushi was gutted salted fish wrapped around fermented rice. Back then the rice wasn't meant to be eaten but as a form of preservation. Doing this gave the fish a sour flavor which is where the name came from.

After a bit of time sushi found its place in America, with refrigeration sushi was able to last much longer than before. Not only that but sushi in America saw significant alterations with the addition of cream cheese and avocado.
